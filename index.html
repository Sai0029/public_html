Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our exam platform? Check out the answers below!

  • What types of exams can be conducted on your platform?

    Our platform supports both objective and coding exams. Whether you need to assess multiple-choice questions or coding skills, we've got you covered.

  • Is proctoring available for exams conducted on your platform?

    Yes, we offer proctoring services to ensure the integrity of exams conducted on our platform. Our proctoring features include live monitoring, AI-based behavior analysis, and more.

  • Can colleges and companies customize exam settings on your platform?

    Absolutely! We provide flexibility for institutions to customize exam settings according to their requirements. From setting time limits to choosing question types, administrators have full control over the exam parameters.

  • Is it possible to integrate our existing learning management system (LMS) with your platform?

    Yes, we offer seamless integration with various LMS platforms. Our API allows for easy integration, enabling institutions to streamline their exam processes and enhance the overall learning experience.

  • Can you develop a custom solution for our specific exam requirements?

    Absolutely! We specialize in developing custom solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Whether you have specific feature requests or integration requirements, our team is here to help you achieve your goals.
